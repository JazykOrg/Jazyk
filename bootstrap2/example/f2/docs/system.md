# Orderly

Orderly is an online shop system. A Customer browses the [Catalog](./catalog.md),
places an [Order](./orders.md), pays through [Payment](./payment.md), and receives a
[Shipment](./shipping.md). Unwanted goods go back through [Returns](./returns.md).

The warehouse side is covered by [Inventory](./inventory.md). Operators manage the
system with the [Admin CLI](./admin.md). Shared terms live in the
[Glossary](./glossary.md) and future plans in the [Roadmap](./roadmap.md).

## Principles

The system shall keep every Order traceable from placement to delivery or return.
When any component rejects a request, the system shall tell the Customer why.
